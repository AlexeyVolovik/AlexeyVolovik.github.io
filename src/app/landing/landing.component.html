We are efficient
Our convoys depart regularly. Thanks to our agreements with the State Border Guards, our vehicles are not unloaded at the border and proceed through customs with no delays. All aid goes directly to where it’s needed right now.
We have support
We have support letters from The Office of the Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, the State Border Guard Service of Ukraine, and the Office of the President of Ukraine who endorse our mission.
We connect
We are in direct contact with Ukrainian hospitals, government offices, and diplomatic missions. We partner with local officials and volunteers, as well as humanitarian aid organisations and groups in Europe.
We optimise
We have deals with humanitarian organisations that provide the most costly medical devices free of charge. Our outreach to hospitals and suppliers across the EU allow us to source additional medical air as charitable donations.
